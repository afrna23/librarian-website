About the Community Library

Our library is more than books. It’s a future-ready community hub built to support learning, creativity, and growth for both students and adults. We blend traditional resources with smart technology to make information more accessible and exciting.

📖 RFID Book Tracking

Fast and efficient self-checkout with RFID technology — no more waiting in line.

💡 Smart Study Rooms

Private spaces with smartboards, charging stations, and noise control features.

🌐 Digital Access Zone

Free access to online journals, e-books, virtual libraries, and learning platforms.

🤝 Community Corner

Host meetups, workshops, and collaborative events for the public.
